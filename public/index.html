About Conect-R

Conect-R is a full-service digital ecosystem built for restaurants and local businesses. We build websites, NFC-powered digital portals, and connected brand experiences — all integrated with our own software suite: Chamba for team and workforce management, TableReserve for reservations, and NextUp for waitlist and guest flow. We also offer hands-on consulting for restaurants ready to modernize their operations. Everything connects. Everything works together.
